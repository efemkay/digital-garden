title: gmail shortcuts i use
---
- with the focus on the main inbox page, i use the following shortcuts

|  shortcut     |  action                         |
|:--------------|:--------------------------------|
|  x            |  select/unselect conversation   |
|  v            |  open "Move to..." menu         |
| # (shift + 3) | to delete selected conversation |
